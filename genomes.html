This page provides access to parasitic plant genome assemblies generated as part of our research.

Castilleja foliolosa
Woolly Indian Paintbrush
Host range: Generalist
A hemiparasitic plant native to California and Baja California. This genome was sequenced to study strigolactone-induced haustorium formation.
GenBank:
GCA_046119335.1
BLAST:
Protein BLAST
Publication:
Bürger et al., 2024
Orobanche gracilis
Slender Broomrape
Host range: Fabaceae
A holoparasitic plant that exclusively parasitizes members of the legume family. Features expanded protein-degrading and carbohydrate-active enzyme families.
GenBank:
GCA_050575165.1
BLAST:
Protein BLAST
Publication:
Bürger et al., 2025
Orobanche hederae
Ivy Broomrape
Host range: Araliaceae
A holoparasitic plant highly specialized to parasitize Araliaceae, mostly ivy. Despite morphological similarity to O. minor, it has a much narrower host range.
GenBank:
GCA_050575145.1
BLAST:
Protein BLAST
Publication:
Bürger et al., 2025
Orobanche minor
Common Broomrape
Host range: Generalist
A holoparasitic generalist capable of parasitizing numerous host species across multiple plant families.
